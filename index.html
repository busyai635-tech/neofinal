FABRICATION LANE

HOW KARTS ARE BORN

STEP 01

CHROMOLY FRAMES

We start with raw high-tensile molybdenum-chrome alloy tubes, bent to exact dimensions on custom tooling jigs.

STEP 02

TIG HAND WELDING

Master welding engineers fusion TIG-weld the chassis framework to avoid micro-fractures under massive side G-forces.

STEP 03

POWERPLANT INSTALL

We mount high-performance 4-stroke petrol engines or eco-friendly electric drivetrains with automatic wet clutch systems.

STEP 04

AERO SHIELDS

Impact-resistant plastic side pods and carbon-fiber front noses are fitted to lower air drag coefficients.

STEP 05

TRACK TESTING

Every kart undergoes extensive dyno tests, throttle response calibration, brake force balancing, and alignment checks before leaving the factory.

STEP 06

TRACK DISPATCH

Shipped securely with high-density protective frames directly to rental arenas or championship paddocks.

THE RACING ARCHITECTURE

200cc 4-Stroke Engine

⚙️

Reliable Honda-type four-stroke engine offering 9 HP. Features an automatic wet clutch and heavy-duty chain drive for robust power output.

Heavy-Duty Chassis

🛠️

TIG-welded spaceframe framework designed for optimal torsional stiffness and maximum impact safety under race conditions.

High-Grip Slick Tires

⭕

Racing slick rubber compound engineered to maximize track grip, providing predictable cornering limits and long tread life.

Direct Steering Linkage

🎯

Simple, direct mechanical linkage geometry offering heavy, instantaneous feedback for surgical steering precision.

Self-Ventilating Brakes

🛑

Hydraulic rear disc brakes with self-ventilating rotors for reliable stopping power and minimal thermal fade on long race sessions.
